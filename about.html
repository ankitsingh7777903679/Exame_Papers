About QuestionBanker

Your trusted companion in academic excellence through comprehensive exam preparation.

Our Mission

At QuestionBanker, our mission is to democratize access to quality educational resources. We believe that every student, regardless of their background or resources, should have access to comprehensive study materials to help them excel in their academic pursuits.

By providing a vast collection of previous year question papers, we aim to familiarize students with exam patterns, question types, and marking schemes, ultimately helping them gain confidence and achieve better results.

Our Story

QuestionBanker was founded in 2024 by a group of educators and technology enthusiasts who recognized a critical gap in the availability of structured and easily accessible exam papers.

What started as a small repository of GSEB board exam papers has now grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of students across various boards and college courses.
