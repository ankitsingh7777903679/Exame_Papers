Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request specific exam papers?

You can request specific exam papers by using the feedback form above or by contacting us through our Telegram group.

Are all the papers available for free?

Yes, all exam papers on QuestionBanker are available for free. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.

How often are new papers added?

We update our repository as soon as new papers become available after each exam season. Typically, we add new papers within a week of their release.

Can I contribute to QuestionBanker?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from teachers and students. If you have exam papers that aren't available on our site, please share them through the feedback form.
